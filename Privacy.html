Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 15, 2026 [cite: 2]

Welcome to FlappyPDF (“Company”, “we”, “us”, or “our”). We operate the website located at FlappyPDF.com (the “Service”). [cite: 3]

FlappyPDF is a privacy-first, decentralized multi-tool document utility designed with an innovative hybrid infrastructure. [cite: 4] Our primary file utilities (including image-to-PDF and PDF-to-image conversion) utilize high-leverage client-side execution frameworks (jsPDF, pdfjs-dist). [cite: 5] This means that the processing of your documents happens entirely within your local browser sandbox; [cite: 6] your files are not transmitted, uploaded, or stored on our servers. [cite: 7]

To ensure universal compliance with international data protection frameworks, this Privacy Policy has been structurally optimized to meet the legal mandates of the European Union General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA/CPRA), the Canadian Personal Information Protection and Electronic Documents Act (PIPEDA), the UK Data Protection Act, and other applicable global data privacy regulations. [cite: 8]

1. The Architectural Zero-Trace Principle (Document Processing) [cite: 9]

Unlike legacy cloud-based file convertors that force user uploads to remote servers, FlappyPDF applies a strict Privacy by Design & Default data framework. [cite: 10]

  • Local Execution: When you drag, drop, or select files for core tools, the underlying software files execute via client-side JavaScript. [cite: 11]
  • Zero File Exfiltration: No payloads containing your document data, textual contents, metadata, or embedded graphics are sent over the network to FlappyPDF infrastructure during client-side operations. [cite: 12]
  • The "Offline Test" Guarantee: Users can formally verify this local compliance by loading our conversion utility tool page, completely disconnecting their device from the internet (Airplane Mode), and processing a conversion. [cite: 13] The operation will execute locally with zero network dependencies. [cite: 14]

2. Data We Actually Collect [cite: 15]

While your files remain entirely your own, we do process highly limited categories of information to maintain security, execute authenticated cloud tasks, prevent automated server-abuse, and process subscription transactions. [cite: 16]

A. Personally Identifiable Information (PII) [cite: 17]

We only collect explicit personal information when you intentionally provide it to us. [cite: 18] This occurs when you register an account, purchase a premium subscription, or request support: [cite: 19]

  • Account Infrastructure Data: Email address, user identification tokens, and account display name. [cite: 20] This data is managed securely via our backend architecture (Supabase / PostgreSQL). [cite: 21]
  • Transactional Billing Data: Payment processing is handled strictly through decoupled, PCI-compliant third-party payment gateways (e.g., Stripe). [cite: 22] FlappyPDF never reads, retains, or stores raw credit card details, CVVs, or billing credentials on its own servers. [cite: 23]

B. Automated Operational Log Files [cite: 24]

To manage overall platform stability, prevent malicious traffic spikes (DDoS mitigations), and monitor Core Web Vitals, our servers and global Content Delivery Networks (CDNs) automatically collect limited technical operational metrics: [cite: 25]

  • Internet Protocol (IP) Address (fully anonymized or hashed where legally required). [cite: 26]
  • Browser type, user-agent configuration, and operating system layouts. [cite: 27]
  • Date and time-stamps of network-level connections. [cite: 28]
  • Anonymized telemetry regarding service errors or broken internal operational workers. [cite: 29]

3. Legal Bases for Processing Data (GDPR & International Frameworks) [cite: 30]

If you reside within the European Economic Area (EEA), the United Kingdom, or similar legal jurisdictions, our processing of your restricted personal data relies on clear legal foundations under Article 6 of the GDPR: [cite: 31]

  • Consent: You give us explicit permission to process data (e.g., creating an account or subscribing to our newsletter). [cite: 32]
  • Performance of a Contract: Processing your email address and transaction state is mandatory to deliver premium account tiers and services. [cite: 33]
  • Legitimate Interests: We preserve server security, defend our systems against automated script bots or scrapers, monitor network performance, and optimize our dark-themed minimalist user interface. [cite: 34]

4. Data Retention and Disposal Timelines [cite: 35]

  • User Documents: Because files are processed entirely on the client side, our servers maintain zero (0) seconds of retention. [cite: 36] Your files exist only in your browser’s volatile memory sandbox and are fully discarded the moment the tab or browser window is closed. [cite: 37]
  • Account & Profile Data: We retain your PII (email, profile configurations) for as long as your account remains open. [cite: 38]
  • Account Deletion: You can trigger a full database wipe at any time. [cite: 39] Upon receiving an account deletion request via your user settings console or via legal intake channels, all corresponding profile rows stored inside our database infrastructure are completely and permanently purged within thirty (30) days, in strict compliance with the EDPB Article 17 enforcement standards. [cite: 40]

5. Cookies and Cookie Preferences [cite: 41]

FlappyPDF uses a minimal cookie footprint to avoid consent fatigue while providing an optimal web experience. [cite: 42]

  • Essential Functional Cookies: These are strictly necessary cookies utilized to manage secure user authentication tokens, handle persistent session tracking, and remember your dark theme preferences (#0d0d0f). [cite: 43]
  • Analytics & Performance Web Beacons: We utilize fully privacy-compliant, anonymized telemetry utilities to gauge click patterns, page tracking, and site load speeds. [cite: 44]
  • Opt-Out Mechanisms: In compliance with global frameworks, our cookie infrastructure includes a clear, single-click preference panel letting you opt out of any non-essential cookies. [cite: 45] Your refusal state is strictly honored for a minimum of six consecutive months before asking again. [cite: 46]

6. Transborder Data Transfers & Third-Party Subsurface Processors [cite: 47]

To maintain high global availability and fast loading speeds, FlappyPDF distributes static application resources across international Content Delivery Networks. [cite: 48]

  • Infrastructure Partners: Our primary structural servers, database handlers, and authorization states are powered securely via infrastructure like Supabase and major cloud hosting networks. [cite: 49]
  • Data Safeguards: Whenever any personal metadata moves across international borders (e.g., outside the EEA, UK, or Canada), it is continuously protected by strict technical protocols, including the European Commission’s Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) and matching data protection agreements. [cite: 50]

7. Your Jurisdiction-Specific Privacy Rights [cite: 51]

Regardless of where you reside, FlappyPDF supports comprehensive data autonomy. [cite: 52] You can exercise these rights directly through your account dashboard or by contacting our legal compliance team. [cite: 53]
