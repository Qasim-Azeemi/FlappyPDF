Is my PDF secure during conversion?

▼

Yes. FlappyPDF uses PDF.js, which renders your PDF entirely inside your browser. Your document is never sent to a server — it stays on your device from start to finish.

What's the difference between JPG, PNG, and WEBP?

▼

JPG is best for photos and documents with lots of colour — small file size. PNG is lossless, ideal for sharp text, diagrams, or anything needing transparency. WEBP gives the best balance of quality and small file size, perfect for web use.

What image quality will I get?

▼

Pages are rendered at 2× resolution (scale factor 2), which produces sharp, high-quality images suitable for printing and sharing. Typical output is 150–200 DPI equivalent per page.
